Web Evaluations

clickup.com

Clickerup is a AI meant to optimize workflow for company teams. Overall its job is to reduce time managing and tracking progress for team projects. ClickUp can sort through task, spreadsheets, documents and more.

  • Contrast: Despite using a variety of colors the contrast of the text and background is clear. Mainly since white and black are the primary colors used on this page.
  • Repetition: There's a consistency with the colors used. From cool blues to warm and vibrant pinks despite the variety of colors none of them are overused.
  • Alignment: Categories on each page is well aligned despite the different kinds of alignments (center, left and right) being used for the pictures and main content.
  • Proximity: Information and pictures are correctly grouped together

www.lingscars.com

Lingscars is a website used for leasing various car brands. The website is known as UKs craziest car leasing website.

  • Contrast: Despite the overwhelming amount of colors the website does have some contrast when it comes to its text but large amount of patterns and colors make it hard know what the consumer should be focusing on.
  • Repetition: There are repeated elements used on the page. The problem is that there are too many types o repeating elements cluttering the page.
  • Alignment: The layout has too many dynamic elements, patterns and colors.
  • Proximity: The content is grouped together as seen in the similar colors and pictures used. However, the different types of content is too close making the page feel cluttered.
