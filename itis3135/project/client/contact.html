Commission Inquiry

You will be entered into a queue to see if your commission request is possible at the moment. The commission will be drawn to the sketch phase before requiring half payment.

Explanation on commission: Ordering a commission is like ordering a burger. Choose your ingredients: If you want a colored bust sketch then choose sketch, bust, and flat color. If you want full body line art but no color then only choose line art and full body.

Commission Details
Benefits
  • You will be allowed revisions as long as the art isn't past the sketch phase.
  • If you have ordered colors as well, you will be granted 3 revisions from your previously selected color theme.
  • Any revisions made past the finished commission will cost extra.
  • Be ready to give me some references (pictures) when I contact you for what you want for your commission for greater accuracy. References are not required.
  • Payment can be processed via Paypal.
