Project Name: ChillyHERMIT Portfolio Collab

Project Overview:

Application is meant to inform potential clients of Chillycrab’s services. The users of the website would be those who are interested in the art of Chillycrab and wish to contact her about a potential commission regarding:
- Streaming overlays, panels, tubers, emotes
- Fanart
- Game art
- Any art related projects (Books, games and prints)
All purchases are digitally only as she specializes in digital art.

Website would include:

  • A homepage with her logo present, a small introduction and her contact information
  • A Gallery showing fully rendered art
  • A Project section showing the current projects she is focusing on
  • A Pricing page to give the customers an idea of what they can purchase, how much it costs and rules regarding what she will make
  • A contact sheet listing all the ways to contact her if interested in a commission/project

Possible Users:

  • Customers (Visiting to buy prints or commission artwork)
  • Partners (Visiting website to look through Project Progress or contact information)
  • Artists (Visiting to look through art gallery or contact me for a partnership)
